Make sequence

Creates a sequence, calling cs_seq_make_sequence_ll internally.

Parameters:
offset what this sequencer considers time zero.
length the duration of this sequence.
sequence_length the length of the sequence argument.
sequence a sequence of triples, [start, end, value] indicating the sequence to play.
repeat whether the sequence immediately repeats when finished or stops. If there is a sequence queued, this value is ignored.
